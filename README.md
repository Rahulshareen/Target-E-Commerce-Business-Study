# Target-E-Commerce-Business-Study
Below is the analysis on Target E commerce Data:

Analyzed 100K+ orders across 4,119 cities and 27 states, identifying business growth from 2016 to 2018.

Discovered strong YoY growth with orders increasing over 100x (2016–2017) and revenue rising by ~136.98% (2017–2018).

Identified clear monthly seasonality and peak customer activity in the afternoon.

Highlighted key markets, with one state contributing ~42% of total customers and others showing low penetration.

Evaluated delivery performance, identifying early deliveries, delays, and regional inefficiencies in logistics.

Utilized data query tools such as MySQL and BigQuery to extract and analyze large datasets, resulting in a 25% reduction in data retrieval time and enabling faster decision-making for business strategies.

Tools Used: SQL,BigQuerry
