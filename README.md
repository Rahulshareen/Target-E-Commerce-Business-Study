# Target-E-Commerce-Business-Study

🎯 Target Brazil — E-Commerce SQL Analysis

🔍 Key Findings

Order growth: 45,101 orders in 2017 → 54,011 in 2018 (+20% YoY). Jan–Aug revenue comparison shows +137% growth ($3.67M → $8.69M)
Geography: SP, RJ, MG account for 66.5% of all customers — three states drive two-thirds of the business
Peak ordering window: 38% of orders placed 13:00–18:00 — afternoon is the highest-conversion window
Delivery accuracy: 91.9% of orders delivered before estimated date, averaging 11.9 days ahead of ETA
Freight disparity: Northern states (RR, PB, RO) pay 2–3× more in freight than SP — a direct demand suppressor
Payment behaviour: 73.9% credit card usage; 50.6% of customers pay in a single installment


💡 Business Recommendations

Concentrate logistics and marketing investment in SP, RJ, MG before expanding to smaller states
Schedule promotions between 13:00–18:00 BRT to align with peak purchase behaviour
Introduce freight subsidies in northern states to unlock suppressed demand
Replace inflated delivery ETAs in remote areas with accurate estimates to build customer trust
Explore a co-branded credit card programme to convert 73.9% card preference into platform loyalty
